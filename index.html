The Saviour

In an enveloped reality void of normal logic or consequences; characters controlled by people in our reality are loaded on a flying bus and made to disembark onto an island. The last person alive is the player considered the "winner" so everyone is in a "shoot on sight" mood. The island has numorous towns, a farm, a junkyarad, a lake, a junkyard, and other things of the such filled with various lethal firearms and weapons. Everyone was paired up with someone else in a cooperative effort to win. My counterpart is always Daniel Campbell.
